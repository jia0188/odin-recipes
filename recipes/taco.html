Simple taco shell

Welcome to the Playground, we are all chefs here.

This corn tortillas taco shell is homemade and oil free.

Ingredients needed

  • 2 cups (240 grams) Corn flour (Masa harina)
  • 1.5 to 1.75 cup Hot water
  • 3/4 teaspoon Fine sea salt

Steps

  1. Mix the dough. Briefly whisk the masa harina and salt together, then gradually stir in 1.5 cups of the hot water until an evenly-mixed dough begins to form. Use your hands to knead the dough for 2-3 minutes in the mixing bowl
  2. Rest the dough: let it rest for about 10 minutes
  3. Portion the dough: scoop a 2 tablespoon ball of dough, then use your hands to roll the ball until it is nice and round
  4. Cook the tortilla. Cook the tortilla for about 40-60 seconds per side, flipping it once speckled brown spots begin to appear on the bottom of the tortilla.
  5. Serve with your favorite fillings!
