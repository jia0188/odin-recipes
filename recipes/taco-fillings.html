Taco Fillings

There are a few options that you may choose as your vegan meat.

Some of the options are: tofu, jackfruit, textured soy protein...etc

To make our lives easier here, I have opted to hunt our own meat at the supermarket. In the vegan aisle, of course.

Ingredients needed

  • 1 lb Beyond Meat
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 oregano, minced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup lettuce, shredded
  • 1/2 bunch cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 4 limes, sliced
  • 1/2 cup vegan cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 8 soft corn tortillas

Steps

  1. In a large saut√© pan, over medium heat, add Beyond Beef and cook for 5 minutes.
  2. Add tomato sauce and seasoning. Simmer for about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Heat the corn tortillas until slightly browned.
  4. Add the mix onto the corn tortillas.
  5. Garnish with cheddar cheese, avocado, lettuce, cilantro, tomato, and lime.
  6. Serve!
